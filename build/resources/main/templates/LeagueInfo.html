Teams Overview

Managers may select a starting lineup from these eight (8) players. The chart below lists the required number of players at each position for the 'starting lineup' and the max that can be 'drafted'.

Roster Requirements
Position Starting Lineup Draft
Guard (G) 2 4
Forward (F) 2 4
Center (C) 2 4
Util (G|F|C) 2 -
All Positions 8 8


During the draft, players are drafted at the position they are listed under in our database.


How Lineups are Scored


Scoring
Statistic Value
PTS - Points 1 point
REB - Rebounds 1.2 points
AST - Assists 1.5 points
BLK - Blocks 2 points
STL - Steals 2 points


Each NBA player's points will be averaged based on the game played. For example, if LeBron James scores 90 Fantasy points in 3 games, he will only receive points for the game you selected.
