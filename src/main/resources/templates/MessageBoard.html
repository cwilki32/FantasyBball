Message Board

Rules

1. Criticize Play, Not Player. Critical comments about UNC sports are allowed. Personal attacks on players, coaches, recruits are not. 2. Discuss Post, Not Poster. This prevents discussions from getting personal. Attacking other posters will not be tolerated. 3. Watch The Language. Profanity or other inappropriate language is not acceptable. Profanity abbreviations and acronyms are also not permitted. 4. Stay On Topic. No politics, no religion on the sports message boards. Racial, ethnic, homophobic and sexual slurs are grounds for immediate dismissal. 5. Threats = Ban. Any form of physical threat (even in jest) against a fellow poster or public figure can be grounds for an immediate banning. 6. Don't Spam. You're entitled to your opinion, but keep it to relevant threads. If you take it to unrelated threads, that will be considered spamming an agenda, which derails threads. 7. Don't Bait. Do not try to irritate other posters or stir up a controversial topic. This is a community.
